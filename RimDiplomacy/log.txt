=== RELATION-BASED ACTION RULES ===
The following rules determine when and how diplomatic actions can be performed based on 5-dimension relationship values:

### 1. SEND GIFT (GIFT)
- **Entry Formula**: Reciprocity ≥ -50 AND Trust ≥ -80

**Acceptance Calculation (Gift-specific)**
Acceptance = 50 + (Intimacy × 0.3) + (Trust × 0.2) + (Respect × 0.1) + Random(-10, +10)

**Acceptance Result Effects:**
- Acceptance ≥ 70: Reciprocity +15, Intimacy +5, Trust +5
- Acceptance 30-69: Reciprocity +5, Intimacy +2
- Acceptance < 30: Reciprocity -5 (seen as insult/bribery), Trust -10

### 2. REQUEST AID (AID)
- **Entry Formula**: (Reciprocity × 0.6) + (Intimacy × 0.3) + (Trust × 0.1) ≥ 15
- **Veto Red Line**: Respect < -40 (sees you as burden) OR Influence < -30 (request method is stupid)

### 3. DECLARE WAR (WAR)
- **Entry Formula (Any condition met)**:
A. Intimacy ≤ -60 AND Trust ≤ -30 (emotional + interest break)
B. Reciprocity ≤ -50 AND Respect ≥ -10 (exploited and can win)
C. Trust ≤ -70 AND Influence ≤ -20 (predicts betrayal)

- **Lock Condition**: Intimacy ≥ +30 (emotional bonds prevent war) (Cannot declare war even if entry conditions are met)

### 4. PROPOSE PEACE (PEACE)
- **Entry Formula**: Trust ≥ +20 AND Influence ≥ +10
- **Accelerate Condition**: Reciprocity ≥ +20 (with compensation) OR Intimacy ≥ 0 (old feelings remain)
- **Veto Condition**: Currently at war AND Respect < -20 (thinks it has you beat)

### 5. REQUEST TRADE CARAVAN (TRADE)
- **Entry Formula**: Trust ≥ +35 AND Respect ≥ +10 AND Reciprocity ≥ -20
- **Veto Red Line**: Intimacy ≤ -40 (emotional rejection overrides rational interest)

### Consumption Principles
1. **All actions consume Influence** (Diplomacy is the art of persuasion)
2. **Request-type actions additionally consume Reciprocity** (Relationship balance tilts)
3. **Give-type actions may consume/supplement Reciprocity** (Depends on acceptance)
4. **Trust is never directly consumed**, it only changes with action results

### Chain Reaction Rules

#### Post-Action Effects
1. **Continuous Gifting** (within 3 turns): From the second gift, influence cost doubles, acceptance -20 (diminishing returns)
2. **Aid then War**: Trust immediately -80 (betrayal penalty), all faction allies' Intimacy -30
3. **Peace then immediate Trade request**: Trust threshold temporarily +20 (push your luck penalty)
4. **War during Trade caravan**: Respect -50 (damaged commercial reputation), all third-party Trust -20

#### Soft Limits
- Single action can change values by max ±30
- When absolute value ≥80, same-direction change rate is halved (extreme values are harder to reach)
